Game Play and Hints
Objectives: You, a traveler find an entity in need. Help it enter its home!
- make use of hints you can find by clicking on objects on the screen
- there are a total of two hints
- to access the hint, you will need to complete a mini challenge


Conclusion:
The game is over when you find the final method to enter the house. You must be creative.
Click on the entrance point you see fit based on the clues.
(for example, if a clue shows a key, find a key and click on the door.)

Good luck, traveler!
hint one: The trees bring life to us; with this we do what we must.
